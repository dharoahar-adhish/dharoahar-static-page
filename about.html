About Dharoahar

Dharoahar is committed to delivering premium Indian spices sourced directly from the finest origin farms across India. Our carefully curated selection ensures authenticity, quality, and consistency in every batch.

Our Sourcing Regions:

Kerala Spices: Our clove, cardamom, cinnamon, black pepper, cumin powder, garam masala, and turmeric are sourced from Kerala's lush plantations, known for exceptional quality and aroma

Rajasthan Spices: Our cumin and coriander powder come directly from Rajasthan, India's spice capital, ensuring authentic earthy flavors

Andhra Pradesh Chilies: Our degi chili and Kashmiri chili are sourced from Guntur, delivering vibrant color and authentic heat
